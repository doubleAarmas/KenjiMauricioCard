Kenji Mauricio

Only found in the lands known as Westchester in Miami, Florida. This creature is fiercely loyal to his owners and has an overwhelming need to follow his father directly behind him wherever he may be heading.

  • Favorite Food: Cheez-its
  • Favorite Pasttime: Sleeping or eating
  • Average lifespan: 30 years
  • General Temperament: Extremely loyalty towards those brave enough to feed it but otherwise a lazy sleepy boy

This particular Snorkie was saved from a home that was overcrowded with more of his kind. The previous owners no longer able to care for him passed him along to our family. Little did we know he would be known throughout the block due to escaping if the front door is ever cracked open and always know to return home where he is fed and pampered. Neighbors with outdoor cats beware!
